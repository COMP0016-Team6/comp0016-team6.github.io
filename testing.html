Unit and Integration Testing

We adopted mock testing that virtual some objects which are not easy to construct or obtain for testing.

And basically, every test should create a mock require and response first and then check the response of its output.

As a result, except for a few completely unreachable branches (both for developers and users), the coverage of the remaining branches is 100%.
